Shower & Supplies Adventure

Each day brings a new challenge. Adapt and serve the community!

How to Play:

  • Click "Accept Supply Drop" then drag supplies to the correct bins below.
  • Drag waiting guests (😊) to an available shower.
  • Click dirty showers (🧹) to clean them.
  • After showering, guests will ask for specific items. Click the item's icon on their request to give it to them.
  • Serve guests before they become angry (😠)! Watch the Daily Update for new challenges.
